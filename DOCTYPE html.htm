        <h2>Diet and Nutrition Management</h2>

        <p>Here at Wild Space, we prioritize the health and well-being of our harbour seals through carefully formulated diets.</p>

        <h3>Formulated Diet:</h3>

        <ul>

            <li>Fish (such as herring, mackerel, and squid)</li>

            <li>Shrimp</li>

            <li>Clams</li>

            <li>Vegetables (such as carrots and leafy greens) for enrichment</li>

        </ul>

        <p>Our diet provides a balanced nutrition profile tailored to meet the specific needs of harbour seals. Regular analysis ensures that it meets the required nutrient content and energy levels.</p>

        <h3>Feeding Schedule:</h3>

        <p>Harbour seals are fed multiple times throughout the day, mimicking their natural feeding behaviors.</p>

        <h3>Benefits of the Diet:</h3>

        <p>Our formulated diet promotes optimal health and behavior among our harbour seals, ensuring they receive the necessary nutrients for growth, energy, and immune function.</p>

        <h3>Ease of Management:</h3>

        <p>The ingredients for our diet are readily available, and the preparation process is straightforward. We prioritize cost-effectiveness and efficient storage to streamline our feeding protocols.</p>

    </section>

   

    <!-- Preventative Health Section -->

    <section id="preventative-health">

        <h2>Preventative Health</h2>

        <p>At Wild Space, we implement comprehensive preventative health measures to safeguard the well-being of our harbour seals.</p>

        <h3>Key Considerations:</h3>

        <ul>

            <li>Regular health check-ups by veterinarians</li>

            <li>Hygiene protocols for staff members</li>

            <li>Quarantine procedures for new arrivals</li>

        </ul>

        <h3>Infectious Diseases:</h3>

        <p>We are vigilant against common infectious diseases among harbour seals and implement strict biosecurity measures to prevent transmission.</p>

        <h3>Non-Infectious Health Problems:</h3>

        <p>Our staff members are trained to identify and mitigate non-infectious health risks, such as injuries or stress-related issues.</p>

        <h3>Preventative Actions:</h3>

        <p>Regular monitoring, proper nutrition, and environmental enrichment are key to preventing health issues among our harbour seals.</p>

    </section>

   

    <!-- Enclosure Design Section -->

    <section id="enclosure-design">

        <h2>Enclosure Design</h2>

        <p>Our harbour seal enclosures are thoughtfully designed to promote optimal health and husbandry.</p>

        <h3>Suitable Design:</h3>

        <p>Our enclosures feature spacious pools with naturalistic elements to simulate the seals' native habitat.</p>

        <h3>Water Parameters:</h3>

        <p>We maintain strict control over water parameters, including temperature, pH, and salinity, to ensure the health of our seals.</p>

        <h3>Life Support Systems:</h3>

        <p>State-of-the-art filtration systems and regular monitoring ensure the quality and cleanliness of the water.</p>

        <h3>Construction:</h3>

        <p>We use durable materials and efficient layout designs to facilitate easy cleaning and maintenance.</p>
